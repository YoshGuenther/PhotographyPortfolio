Using a third party

*phone vibrates*

As I open up my groupme app, I am expecting to find myself having to look through a massive feed of messages that I have missed. "If anyone is interested, such and such org needs a photographer tonight..." My interest is sparked. I love photographing events. While it can be very challenging (imagine photographing a scavenger hunt across Austin), I am always intrigued by the people that I have the pleasure of meeting.

Often enough, I find people who are looking for events to be photographed last minute. While I am happy to volunteer my time to document them, it can become very frustrated when you have to communicate through people to get information about an event.

If you want a photographer, especially one to do it for free, please have the courtesy to contact them ahead of time.

One of the best traits I have seen in photographers is remaining patient. Whether you are struggling to get a dog to sit still or being patient has its rewards.
