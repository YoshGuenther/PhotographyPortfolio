Jason and Kathryn
Guenther's Maternity

May 3rd, 2016

Meet Jason and Kat! I had the pleasure of taking maternity photos for this blessed couple. There baby boy is expected to arrive mid-May and their family couldn't be more excited. Even their dog, Kylie, wanted to get in on the action and have her photo taken.

Self Critique:

Lighting:

I started late into the Golden Hour, so the light was fading fast. As a result, you can clearly see the photos get darker as the shoot pogressed. Also, this caused the shoot be very short - it was under 30 minutes. I also could have used a reflector to remove facial shadows.

Focus:

I was in such a rush that I didn't look at my playback screen often enough to make sure I was getting the shots I wanted. As a result, there were several shots that were out of focus and had to be tossed. Additionally, I failed to capture Kat's beautiful brown eyes clearly in the photo where she is leaning backward in the wheel barrow, looking at the camera.

Creativity:

Since this was my first maternity shoot, and second portrait shoot overall, I feel that I did a good job of coming up with ideas on the spot. For some of them, I built off other ideas that I have seen before, such as the water hose. However, I also played on the personality of the couple. Jason is very goofy and enjoys working with his hands. After scavenging through his workshop, I came up with the idea to put Kat in a wheel barrow.
