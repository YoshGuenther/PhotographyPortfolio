Rebecca in the rain

May 14th, 2016

Meet my beautiful girlfriend Rebecca! When she is not studying to be a vet at Texas A & M, she often comes to visit me in Austin. During her stay, she helps me get better at photography by patiently posing for me. Today I had the oppertunity to practice shooting in the rain with her.

Self Critique

Lighting:

The lighting varied from location to location. The overcast skys helped by diffusing the light. However, some of the locations where shaded and caused shadows along the brow/eyes. I used a 3 foot golden reflector on a stand to cast a warm look on Rebecca's face in several of the photos.

Focus:

I had my camera in a protective rain sleeve and as a result, it was very difficult to look through the viewfinder and ensure that the focus was positioned correctly.

Creativity:

I was really happy with the shoot, as I used a variety of angles and proximity that helped magnify the shots. Additionally, the rainfall and wet surfaces created a whimsical surrounding.
