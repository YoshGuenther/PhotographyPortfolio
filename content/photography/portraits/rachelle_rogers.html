Rachelle Rogers

August 7th, 2016

Rachelle is a Product Management Intern working at Visa this summer.

Self Critique

Lighting:

The indoor lighting, that was often directly overhead, made it difficult to evenly light the face. This would been a good time to use a flash, but I didn't have mine.

Settings:

Appropriate use of aperature and focal point.

Creativity:

I did my research ahead of time and found several locations around the Visa Austin campus. Since this was at a coorpate campus, I attempted to hide the buildings by adjust my angle. I wasn't sure of the poses that I would be using before the shoot. All in all, we came up with poses on the spot, using continues actions to make the shots look more candid.
